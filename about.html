The Story Behind Affordin

Affordin began with a simple but powerful idea: everyone deserves a space to call home, a space that's not only affordable, but smart, sustainable, and designed for real life. Founded in 2025 by Yizhe, Affordin was born out of frustration with rising housing costs and the lack of flexible living options. We saw families squeezed into overpriced rentals, students juggling unstable rent, and workers commuting hours just to afford shelter. So we asked: What if home could be reimagined?

Our answer was the Affordin, tech-enhanced living unit built for comfort, efficiency, and accessibility. Each unit is equipped with modern technology: smart climate control, energy-efficient lighting, and seamless connectivity.
